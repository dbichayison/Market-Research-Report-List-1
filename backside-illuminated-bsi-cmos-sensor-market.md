<p><strong>The Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS</li><li>Active Pixel Sensor CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Backside-Illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Passive Pixel Sensor (PPS) CMOS and Active Pixel Sensor (APS) CMOS. PPS CMOS utilizes simpler pixel structures, making it cost-effective for low-resolution applications, but may compromise image quality. In contrast, APS CMOS features individual amplification for each pixel, enhancing image quality, dynamic range, and video capabilities. This technology is preferred in high-performance imaging applications like smartphones, cameras, and industrial systems, driving demand for advanced imaging solutions.</p></p>
