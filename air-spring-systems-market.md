<p><p>Air spring systems are classified into several types, each serving specific applications and requirements. Convoluted air spring systems feature a series of convolutions that provide flexibility and strength, ideal for heavy-duty vehicles. Sleeve air spring systems consist of a cylindrical rubber sleeve, allowing for greater travel and load support, commonly used in lighter vehicles. Other types may include rolling lobe and square air springs, catering to specialized needs across diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications.</p></p>
