<p><p>The solvent waterproof coating market is categorized into liquid and dry waterproof coatings. Liquid waterproof coatings are applied as a fluid and offer seamless protection against moisture, making them ideal for diverse surfaces. They are easy to apply and penetrate well, ensuring uniform coverage. In contrast, dry waterproof coatings typically come as powders or solid materials that require mixing with solvents or water before application. These coatings can provide robust, long-lasting protection but may require more preparation and drying time.</p></p>
