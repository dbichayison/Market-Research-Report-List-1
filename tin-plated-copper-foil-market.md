<p><p>The tin-plated copper foil market primarily consists of two types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced through a mechanical process that compresses copper into thin sheets, offering excellent uniformity and strength, making it suitable for various applications. In contrast, electrolytic copper foil is created by an electroplating process, resulting in finer granularity and improved flexibility. Both types serve crucial roles in electronics, automotive, and other industries where conductivity and corrosion resistance are essential.</p></p>
