<p><p>The mobile phone battery market comprises several types, primarily including Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd), Nickel-Hydrogen (NiMH), and Lithium-Ion (Li-ion) batteries. NiCd batteries, though durable, suffer from memory effect and environmental concerns. NiMH batteries offer improved capacity and are more environmentally friendly, but their energy density is lower than Li-ion. Li-ion batteries dominate the market due to their high energy density, lightweight design, and longer cycle life, making them the preferred choice for modern smartphones and portable devices.</p></p>
