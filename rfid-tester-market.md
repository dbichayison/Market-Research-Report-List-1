<p><strong>The RFID Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency RFID Tester</li><li>High Frequency RFID Tester</li><li>Ultra-High Frequency RFID Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID tester market is segmented into three main types: Low Frequency (LF) RFID testers, High Frequency (HF) RFID testers, and Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) RFID testers. LF RFID testers operate at frequencies up to 125 kHz, commonly used for access control and animal tracking. HF RFID testers, operating around 13.56 MHz, are often seen in contactless payment systems and library applications. UHF RFID testers, functioning between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, enable longer read ranges and are widely used in supply chain management and inventory tracking.</p></p>
