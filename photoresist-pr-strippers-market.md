<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li><li>Negative Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist strippers market is divided into two main types: positive and negative photoresist strippers. Positive photoresist strippers are designed to remove photoresist materials that become soluble when exposed to light, effectively cleaning up during semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, negative photoresist strippers work with materials that become insoluble upon light exposure, requiring different chemical formulations to achieve effective removal. Both types are essential for the manufacturing processes in electronics, aiming for high precision and surface quality in microfabrication.</p></p>
