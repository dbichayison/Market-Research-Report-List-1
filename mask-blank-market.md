<p><p>Mask blanks are essential in photolithography, particularly in semiconductor production. Low Reflectance Chrome-film Mask Blanks minimize reflected light, reducing unwanted interference during exposure and enhancing pattern fidelity. Conversely, Attenuated Phase Shift Mask Blanks utilize phase-shifting techniques to improve resolution by manipulating light waves. Both types serve to enhance imaging accuracy in advanced manufacturing processes, catering to the evolving needs in semiconductor technology, thereby enabling the fabrication of smaller, more efficient electronic components.</p></p>
