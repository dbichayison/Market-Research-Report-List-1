<p><p>Metal detection security doors are categorized into two main types: single zone and multi-zone systems. Single zone metal detectors scan a single area, allowing for a straightforward detection process and are typically used in lower-risk environments. In contrast, multi-zone metal detectors utilize multiple sensor zones to detect metal objects at various heights and locations, providing enhanced security for high-risk areas. These systems offer greater accuracy and detection capabilities, making them suitable for airports, government buildings, and large public venues.</p></p>
