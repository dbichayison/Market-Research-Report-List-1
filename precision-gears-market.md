<p><p>The precision gears market is segmented into various types based on their design and application. Spur gears feature straight teeth and are ideal for parallel shaft applications. Helical gears offer better load capacity and reduced noise due to their angled teeth. Bevel gears enable power transmission between intersecting shafts, while worm gears provide high torque in compact spaces. Gear racks convert rotational motion into linear motion, and "Others" includes specialized gear types. Each type serves distinct industrial needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
