PBI filament is a continuous strand of fiber known for its exceptional thermal resistance and mechanical strength, making it ideal for protective clothing and aerospace applications. In contrast, PBI staple fiber consists of shorter lengths and is often used in applications requiring bulk and softness, like upholstery and insulation. Both types harness PBI’s unique properties, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
