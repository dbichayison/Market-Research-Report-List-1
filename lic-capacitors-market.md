<p><ul><li>Radial Type</li><li>Laminating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LIC capacitors market is categorized into two main types: Radial Type and Laminating Type. Radial Type capacitors feature leads positioned at the same end, making them ideal for compact circuit board applications due to their space efficiency. In contrast, Laminating Type capacitors are designed with multiple layers for enhanced performance and thermal management, often used in applications requiring high capacitance and reliability. Both types cater to diverse needs across electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors, contributing to their growing demand.</p></p>
