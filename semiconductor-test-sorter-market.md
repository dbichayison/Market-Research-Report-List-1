<p><p>The semiconductor test sorter market comprises three main types: Gravity-Feed Test Sorters, which use gravitational force to position and channel devices for testing; Turret Test Sorters, characterized by their rotating mechanisms that facilitate rapid testing and high throughput; and Pick-and-Place Test Sorters, which employ robotic arms to selectively pick and test devices with precision. Each type offers unique advantages in handling, speed, and efficiency, catering to varying manufacturing needs and production scales within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
