<p><p>The medium molecular weight epoxy resin market is categorized into three viscosity types: low, medium, and high viscosity epoxy resins. Low viscosity epoxy resins offer excellent fluidity and are ideal for applications requiring deep penetration, such as coatings and adhesives. Medium viscosity resins strike a balance between ease of handling and performance, suitable for structural applications. High viscosity epoxy resins provide superior thickness, enhancing their use in heavy-duty applications like composites and industrial bonding, offering excellent mechanical properties and durability.</p></p>
