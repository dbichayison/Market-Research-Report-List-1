<p><p>The Integrated X-ray Sources market consists primarily of two types: open type and sealed type. Open type sources allow direct access to the X-ray tube, facilitating maintenance and potential replacements. This makes them suitable for industrial and clinical settings, where reliability and safety are paramount.</p></p>
