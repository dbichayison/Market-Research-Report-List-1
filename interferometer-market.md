<p><p>The interferometer market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. Laser Fizeau interferometers measure surface flatness with high precision. Mach-Zehnder interferometers split beams for phase measurement and imaging. Michelson interferometers are widely used for displacement measurement and spectral analysis. Fabry–Perot interferometers enhance resolution in spectroscopy. Sagnac interferometers are utilized for rotation sensing in navigation systems. Twyman-Green laser interferometers specialize in evaluating optical components' quality. Additionally, other specialized interferometer types cater to niche applications across various industries.</p></p>
