Low-pressure UV lamps emit a specific wavelength, making them effective for smaller applications like drinking water purification. Medium-pressure lamps offer broader UV spectrum, suitable for larger volumes, often used in wastewater treatment. High-pressure UV lamps generate intense UV output, effective for rapid processing and large systems.
