<p><ul><li>Passive Heat Sink</li><li>Active Heat Sink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper heatsink market comprises two primary types: passive and active heatsinks. Passive heatsinks rely solely on natural convection to dissipate heat, utilizing copper's excellent thermal conductivity for efficient cooling without the need for additional components. In contrast, active heatsinks incorporate fans or other cooling mechanisms to enhance airflow and effectively manage heat. Both types serve crucial roles in electronic devices, ensuring optimal performance and longevity by managing thermal output in various applications, from consumer electronics to industrial machinery.</p></p>
