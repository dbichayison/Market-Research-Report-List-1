<p><p>The jewelry casting resin market features two primary curing technologies: Digital Light Processing (DLP) curing and LED curing. DLP curing utilizes a digital projector to emit light across a resin layer, curing it layer by layer efficiently and accurately, making it ideal for intricate designs. LED curing, on the other hand, employs specific wavelengths of light emitted by LEDs to cure resin quickly, offering faster processing times and energy efficiency. Both technologies cater to diverse jewelry manufacturing needs.</p></p>
