<p><p>The metal finishing equipment market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Inorganic Metal Finishing** involves treatments using inorganic compounds, such as acids and salts, to enhance surface properties like corrosion resistance and adhesion. </p><p>   </p><p>2. **Organometallic Finishing** employs organometallic compounds, combining organic and metal elements, to achieve unique surface characteristics and improved durability. </p><p>   </p><p>3. **Mixed Metal Finishing** utilizes a combination of both inorganic and organometallic processes, leveraging the strengths of each to optimize surface performance for diverse applications.</p></p>
